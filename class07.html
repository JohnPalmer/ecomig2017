Composite Labor

Labor composed of workers with education beyond high school (\(L_{Ht}\)) or less than college (\(L_{Lt}\))

$$L_t = \left[ \theta_{Ht} L_{Ht}^{\rho_{HL}} + \theta_{Lt} L_{Lt}^{\rho_{HL}} \right]^{1/\rho_{HL}}$$
$$\rho_{HL} = (\sigma_{HL} - 1)/\sigma_{HL}$$

Composite Labor: High Education

High education labor composed of workers with only some college (\(L_{H1t}\)) or college degree (\(L_{H2t}\))

$$L_{Ht} = \left[ \theta_{H_{1}t} L_{H_{1}t}^{\rho_{H}} + \theta_{H_{2}t} L_{H_{2}t}^{\rho_{H}} \right]^{1/\rho_{H}}$$
$$\rho_H = (\sigma_H - 1)/\sigma_H$$

Composite Labor: Low Education

$$\rho_L = (\sigma_L - 1)/\sigma_L$$
